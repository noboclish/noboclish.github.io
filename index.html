Architectural worker from Ireland, currently based in Rotterdam.

Researcher and Archivist - design Erick van Egeraat / Rotterdam. (2021-2022)...........
My role was to organise 40 years of work, from early Mecanoo projects to Erick’s most recent
work in the Netherlands and Moscow. dEvE’s current workload includes a lot of referral and
extension work from past projects, and as such it became imperative to have an up to date
archive which could be used to inform our way forward, and allow us to work efficiently
on extensions to past projects.
In tandem with this, I assisted the design teams in preparing detail documentation for
projects including the Mercury Tower penthouse in Moscow and the Drents Museum in Assen.

Panel Member - Irish Architecture Foundation Graduates Panel / Dublin. (2021-2022).....
I was chosen to be a member of this panel based on talks and events I had organised for
young architectural workers during 2020 and 2021, including a workshop with activists
from the UVW Section for Architectural Workers in the UK.

Exhibition Manager and Interviewer - Mies.TV / Tallinn. (2019).........................
During the Tallinn Architecture Biennale, Mies.TV employed me as local liaison for their
exhibition in the Estonian Academy of Arts. I also wrote and edited video interviews with
them, forming the official Biennale documentary.
